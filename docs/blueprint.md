# **App Name**: Nyay AI

## Core Features:

- Legal Jargon Translator: Upload legal documents and receive AI-powered translations of complex legal terms and clauses into plain, understandable language.
- Risk Identifier: Scan contracts and agreements using AI to flag potentially risky clauses, unfavorable terms, or highlight missing compliance requirements.
- Legal Document Generator: Utilize an AI-powered tool to auto-generate standard legal documents such as NDAs, simple contracts, or basic vendor agreements based on user input.
- Conversational Legal Assistant: Interact with an AI assistant through a conversational interface to ask 'what-if' legal scenarios and receive context-aware, step-by-step guidance on workflows.
- Document Management: Provide a simple interface for users to upload, store, and access their legal documents securely within the application.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme to convey clarity and professionalism, enhancing readability for legal texts.
- Primary color: A rich, deep blue (#24378A) that evokes trust, stability, and intellectual depth.
- Background color: A very subtle, cool-toned off-white (#F9FBFD) to maintain a clean and professional appearance, preventing eye strain during long reading sessions.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear violet (#5E27EA) to highlight key actions, calls-to-action, and interactive elements, representing innovative solutions and precision.
- Headlines and body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for its modern, machined, and objective look, ensuring maximum clarity and readability for legal information.
- Clean, crisp line-art icons that are intuitive and directly represent legal concepts (e.g., shields, documents, chat bubbles, warning signs), maintaining a minimalist yet informative visual language.
- A structured and organized layout with ample white space, prioritizing content hierarchy and ease of navigation, crucial for managing legal documents and information.
- Subtle, smooth transition animations for interface elements and generative AI responses, providing a responsive and sophisticated user experience without distractions.
